Recipe #1: Cereal

  • Time: 2 min.
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
cereal of choice 1.5 cups
milk of choice 1 cup

Step 1: Prepare ingredients and get bowl and spoon ready.
Step 2: Pour cereal into the bowl, then then the milk.
Step 3: Enjoy cereal.

Source: How to Make Cereal

Recipe #2: Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 7 min.
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
bread 2 slices
shredded cheese a handful
butter small chunk

Step 1: Melt the chunk of butter on your pan over medium heat.
Step 2: Place one piece of bread on the pan and sprinkle the cheese over it.
Step 3: Place the other piece of bread to make a sandwich and flip once the origninal piece of bread is golden brown.
Step 4: Once the other piece of bread is golden brown, take off heat and enjoy.

Source: How to Make a Grilled Cheese

Recipe #3: Rice

  • Time: ~30 min.
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
white rice 1 cup
water 1 cup

Step 1: Bring water to a boil in a pot then turn heat down to a simmer.
Step 2: Pour rice in pot, cover, and let sit for ~30 minutes.
Step 3: After water has evaoporated and rice is of a desired texture, take off heat and enjoy.

Source: How to Make Rice
